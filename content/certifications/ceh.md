## CEH - Certified Ethical Hacker

**Issuing Organization**: EC-Council  
**Date Obtained**: August 2020  
**Credential ID**: ECCXXXXXXX

### About

The CEH certification validates skills in ethical hacking, penetration testing, and security assessment methodologies.

### Coverage Areas

- Footprinting and reconnaissance
- Scanning networks
- Enumeration
- System hacking
- Malware threats
- Social engineering
- Evading IDS, firewalls, and honeypots
