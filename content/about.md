# About

## Professional Summary

I am an Information Security Specialist with a strong focus on Red Team operations and offensive security. My expertise lies in penetration testing, vulnerability assessment, exploit development, and adversarial simulation exercises.

## Core Competencies

- **Red Team Operations**: Planning and executing sophisticated red team engagements
- **Penetration Testing**: Web, network, and mobile application security assessments
- **Adversary Simulation**: Mimicking real-world threat actor behaviors and techniques
- **Security Research**: Staying current with latest vulnerabilities and attack vectors
- **Security Tooling**: Proficient with industry-standard penetration testing frameworks and tools

## Philosophy

I believe in ethical hacking as a means to strengthen organizational security. By understanding how attackers think and operate, we can build better defenses and improve overall security posture.
